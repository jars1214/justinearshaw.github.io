About

I’m an engineer with a passion for building reliable systems where hardware and software come together. My experience spans telecommunications, research, and software development. My background combines electronics, embedded systems, robotics, and modern web technologies.

I enjoy hands-on, project-based work, whether that’s bringing up new hardware, developing embedded software in C/C++ and Python, or troubleshooting complex electronic systems.

I’m naturally curious and enjoy solving technical problems, learning new technologies, and contributing to innovative engineering projects that turn ideas into systems that help humans and society.

I enjoy spending time on creative and social activities, which give me a chance to think differently and recharge. I also enjoy staying active by going to the gym or playing team sports, and I value spending time with friends, whether that’s catching up, playing board games, or just enjoying good company. For me, maintaining a balance between work, creativity, and social connection helps keep me motivated and inspired in everything I do.
